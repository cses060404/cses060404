My proposal of the project for CIT230.

Website name: Rubik's Cube Tournament | Rexburg

Purpose

The purpose of this website is to promote everyone know more about rubik's cube and even fall in love with it. Experts have pointed out that by learing how to solve rubik's cube, we can efficiently exercise our brains. If we spend time and learn how to solve rubik's cube, we will have a better chance to have better logics and reactions when we need to solve problems.

Page One - Home

Page one is going to be the home page.It will have a paragraph of greeting to everyone. A video of the world's record holder and some pictures of rubik's cube. Also, all the competition details. It will have a link to the BYU-I school map, a link to the league discriptions, a link to the schedule and a link to the reward and prizes. The most important thing to have on this page is the document for people to download and fill in order to register the competition.

Page Two - Schedule

This page will include the general information of the competition duration.Then, the detail time and dates of different games. It will also include the loction(room number and building) for a specific game. The "lunch" section will have a botton where people can click and go to the plan view of the building.

Page Three - Transportation

These page will have some pictures of BYU-I, including a picture of how to get to BYU-I from the highway, a pictire of direction to building where the competition would happen, and plan view of the building. It will also have some discriptions of the relative location of the building to help people who have never been to BYU-I find the location easier.

Page Four - Rewards

This page is going to list out what kind of reward you can get for different games and levels. Pictures of prizes will be included. It will also provide a link to people who might want to sponsor the game or make donations to the prizes.

Page Five - Contact

This page will have the contact information. The address of the school, phone number and email address to the competition organizer. It will also include a section of "F&Q" about some general questions people might have. It will also provide a table for people who want to ask questions or leave messages to the organizer.
